Company Background

Amazon, one of the world’s largest e-commerce platforms, offers a wide range of products including electronics, fashion, home goods, and more.

Scenario

Amazon has noticed a drop in repeat purchases among new customers, particularly in the fashion segment. While initial acquisition rates are high, many new users are not making subsequent purchases.

1. Business Model Analysis

Amazon caters to a broad audience including individual consumers and businesses. In the fashion segment (launched in 2002), they aim for a diverse shopping experience with features like "Prime Wardrobe" and "StyleSnap."

2. Business Value Proposition

Why should we improve? Benefits
• Repeat purchases affect profitability
• Increases growth of customer base
• Attractive pricing improves UX		 • Increases CLV (Customer Lifetime Value)
• Reduces CAC (Customer Acquisition Costs)
• Competitive advantage
